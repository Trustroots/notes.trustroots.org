Nostroots

Nostroots is the evolution of Trustroots, built on the Nostr protocol — a decentralized, open network for social networking. The result will be a version of Trustroots that is more open, resilient, and user-empowered. Currently in development. We are first working on an application that replaces the hardly used meet functionality.

What can you do?

  • Post notes on a map to share what's happening in a location
  • Subscribe to areas to hear from other community members
  • Own your data — your identity and connections are portable
